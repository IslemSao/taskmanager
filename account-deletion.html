Account Deletion Policy - Task Manager App

Last Updated: July 4, 2025

This document explains how users can delete their accounts and associated data from the Task Manager application.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Task Manager account using any of the following methods:

Method 1: In-App Account Deletion

  1. Open the Task Manager app on your device
  2. Navigate to your Profile section
  3. Look for "Account Settings" or "Privacy Settings"
  4. Select "Delete Account" option
  5. Follow the confirmation prompts to permanently delete your account

Method 2: Email Request

If you're unable to delete your account through the app, you can send an email request to:

Email: support@taskmanager.app

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include: Your registered email address and reason for deletion (optional)

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and profile details
  • Task Data: All tasks, projects, and associated content you've created
  • Collaboration Data: Project invitations and shared content
  • App Preferences: Settings and customization preferences
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and session information

⚠️ Important Notice: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have backed up any important data before proceeding.

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Account access is disabled immediately
  • Within 7 days: Personal data is removed from active systems
  • Within 30 days: All data is permanently deleted from backups and archives

Shared Projects and Collaborations

If you're part of shared projects:

  • Your contributions to shared projects will be anonymized
  • Project ownership will be transferred to other administrators if applicable
  • Your comments and activity history will be removed or anonymized

Data Retention for Legal Compliance

In certain cases, we may retain minimal data for legal compliance, including:

  • Transaction records (if applicable)
  • Data required for fraud prevention
  • Information subject to legal holds

Such data will be retained only as long as legally required and will be securely disposed of thereafter.

Reactivation

Once your account is deleted, it cannot be reactivated. If you wish to use Task Manager again, you will need to create a new account.

Contact Information

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance:

Email: support@taskmanager.app

Response Time: Within 48 hours

Privacy Officer: privacy@taskmanager.app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this account deletion policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

This document complies with Google Play Developer Policy requirements for account deletion and data handling transparency.
